ABOUT ME

Hi I’m Camille, a Melbourne based yoga teacher and meditation leader. I believe in yoga not only as a pursuit of a physical practice, but as a philosophy.



In 2018 after many years of almost daily practice, I decided to take my first yoga teachers trainging course in the beating heart and soul of yoga: Rishikesh, India. Ever since I have been guiding yoga classes in Melbourne, Berlin and abroad.



I love the way yoga builds a strong bond between mind and body. Strengthening your mind and body can help build a bulletproof mindset.



So come join me at the studio and be a part of this beautiful practice and let’s strengthen our mind and body in unison.
